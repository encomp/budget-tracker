BUDGETPILOT — Privacy-First Personal Budget Tracker
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

OPENING THE APP
Option 1 — Double-click: Open the BudgetPilot/index.html file in Chrome,
  Edge, or Safari.

Option 2 — Install as an app (recommended):
  1. Open index.html in Chrome or Edge
  2. Click the install icon (⊕) in the browser address bar
  3. BudgetPilot will appear in your dock, taskbar, or home screen
  4. Launch it like any other app — no browser UI needed

iPad: Open in Safari → tap the Share icon → tap "Add to Home Screen"

YOUR DATA & PRIVACY
All your data is stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB. Nothing is
ever sent to any server. BudgetPilot works fully offline after the first
load. No account required. No subscription. No tracking.

⚠  IMPORTANT: Clearing your browser's site data or switching to a different
browser profile will erase your data. Use the Export button in the app
regularly to save a backup file. You can restore your data at any time
using the Import button.

IMPORTING BANK TRANSACTIONS
Go to the Import view and drop your bank's CSV export file. BudgetPilot
automatically detects your bank and maps the columns. Supported banks:
Chase, Bank of America, Wells Fargo, Citi, Capital One, American Express,
Discover, USAA. Other banks are supported via automatic column detection.

ADDING THEME PACKS
Go to Settings → Appearance and drop a Theme Pack (.json) file into the
upload zone (or click the zone to browse). Theme packs change colors,
fonts, and motion personality. Once added, themes are saved to your
library — no re-uploading required to switch between them.

Theme packs are available as separate listings in the seller's Etsy shop.
